Hi! I'm Karen Rosero.

I am currently pursuing my Ph.D. at the Erik Jonsson School of Engineering and Computer Science, University of Texas at Dallas. I serve as a research assistant at the Multimodal Signal Processing Laboratory, led by Prof. Carlos Busso. Here, my focus lies in developing healthcare solutions using deep learning models.

My expertise areas include multimodal signal processing, facial analysis tools, automatic speech recognition, affective computing, and immersive audio.
